About Me

I started my coding journey in late March of 2022 when I was looking for a new job in Content Moderation. A listing appeared for a "Content Moderation Strategy Analyst" with the only hard requirement being an understanding of SQL, and being confident in my ability to learn, I started to research, study, and practice with SQL on my computer. Within a few days I realized I actually really enjoyed the hands-on problem solving involved in coding, and after passing a few minor certification tests online, I decided to follow my newfound interest to freeCodeCamp where I received my certification in Responsive Web Design with HTML/CSS. Outside of coding, I like to spend my free time playing videogames, volunteering at the local animal shelter, and pestering my friends with photos of rats I find cute.
